📣 Buzz 50%: Douyin Search + Views 25% · RED Notes + Search 15% · Topic Discussion & Engagement 10%

US Market
📊 Sales 50%: Sephora Bestsellers 25% · Ulta Beauty Bestsellers 25%
📣 Buzz 50%: TikTok Search + Views 25% · YouTube Search & Discussion 15% · Post Discussion & Engagement 10%

Rank conversion uses segmented linear mapping (TOP1=100%, TOP5=90%, TOP10=80%, etc.); social buzz is log-normalized by views/engagement; scoring draws exclusively from platform-native data (sales rank, views, note count, engagement), excluding editorial recommendations or magazine lists.
