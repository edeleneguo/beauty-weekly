Makeup Industry News Section 01

GLOBALL'Oréal Signs 50-Year Gucci Beauty License; Coty Exits for ~$400MM&A▾
Kering & L'Oréal sign 50-year Gucci Beauty license; Coty returns rights early for ~$400M compensation. Effective July 1, 2027.
On July 7-9, Coty, Kering, and L'Oréal issued coordinated statements. Coty agreed to return Gucci Beauty license to Kering for ~$400M, ~1 year ahead of the original 2028 expiration. L'Oréal simultaneously signed a 50-year exclusive license for Gucci fragrance and beauty development, manufacturing, and distribution. L'Oréal CEO Nicolas Hieronimus called it "the start of a 50-year journey." Gucci Beauty revenue has grown 60%+ since 2019 (historical reference).
USHenkel Completes OLAPLEX AcquisitionM&A▾
Henkel announced completion of OLAPLEX acquisition on July 7, strengthening North American presence and global hair care portfolio.
Henkel announced on July 7 the completion of its acquisition of premium hair care brand OLAPLEX. CEO Carsten Knobel stated this is "an important cornerstone of our purposeful growth agenda." The acquisition strengthens Henkel's presence in North America and expands its global hair care footprint. OLAPLEX is known for its patented bond-building technology.
CNGivenchy G Le Rouge Satin Lipstick Launches, Sells Out Day OneLaunch▾
Givenchy's new G Le Rouge Satin Lipstick launched July 6 with Zhao Liying. Her shade N05 Warm Nude Apricot sold out day one; 1M+ social media discussions.
On July 6, Givenchy global makeup & fragrance ambassador Zhao Liying appeared in a black blazer + red dress campaign for the G Le Rouge Satin Lipstick. The collection features 15 shades, priced at ¥420/3.4g, with high-shine satin finish and peptide-infused formula. The ambassador's shade N05 became an instant sellout across multiple cities. Social media discussions exceeded 1 million.
CNChinese Fragrance Brands Go Global: ToSummer × Isabel Marant, Documents City CollectionMarket▾
ToSummer × Isabel Marant collab, Documents S8 city-themed collection at airports, 20+ Chinese fragrance brands at international expos.
Per Jing Daily report, Chinese fragrance brands are using cultural storytelling for global expansion. ToSummer collaborated with French brand Isabel Marant on a limited-edition fragrance (Tunisian orange blossom × Chinese jasmine × Himalayan sandalwood). Documents launched its Season 8 collection of 9 city-themed fragrances with immersive airport installations. 20+ Chinese fragrance brands participated in Paris-Shanghai Fragrance Expo 2025 and Esxence Milan in June 2026.
USMegan Thee Stallion Launches First Fragrance "Hot Girl Summer" via UltaLaunch▾
Megan Thee Stallion × Coty debut fragrance "Hot Girl Summer EDP" launched via Ulta Beauty exclusive July 5, in stores July 11. $84/90ml.
Grammy-winning artist Megan Thee Stallion launched her debut fragrance "Hot Girl Summer Eau de Parfum" in partnership with Coty. The scent blends coconut milk, orchid, and vetiver, housed in a sculptural flame-shaped bottle. Priced at $84 for 90ml and $29 for 10ml travel size. Online pre-launch started July 5 via Ulta Beauty, with full US Ulta store rollout on July 11.
CNINTO YOU 2nd-Gen Cushion Blush Launches with Cherry Dual FinishLaunch▾
INTO YOU launched 2nd-gen cushion blush on July 2 with "Cherry Dual Finish" system (matte + shimmer), priced at ¥49.
INTO YOU launched its 2nd-generation cushion blush on July 2, inspired by "Cherry Dualism" with matte and shimmer dual-finish system. The matte version uses professional light-diffusing technology for soft-focus effect; the shimmer version features 40% water-based color fusion for translucent glow. Comes with custom Rubycell puff. Priced at ¥49.
CNBeast ATELIER BEAST 15th Anniversary "THE MIRROR" Fragrance CollectionLaunch▾
Beast's ATELIER BEAST launched 15th anniversary fragrance collection "THE MIRROR" — 8 scents corresponding to 8 fragrance personalities.
Celebrating its 15th anniversary in 2026, Beast launched THE MIRROR (花镜) fragrance collection under ATELIER BEAST. Four poetic couplets structure 8 fragrances: Himalayan Poppy × Kynam, Once in a Blue Moon × Rooted But I Flow, Love is Blind × Love is Over, Oriental Beauty × Peerless Hero. Each scent corresponds to a fragrance personality type. Available now through Beast official channels.
GLOBALEstée Lauder Restructuring: Layoffs Expanded to 10,000, First China-Made PDRN Product LaunchedCapital▾
Estée Lauder restructuring costs surge; layoffs expanded to 10,000. Company also launched first China-made recombinant PDRN product.
Per BeautyTop100 weekly report (July 13), Estée Lauder's restructuring costs have surged, with layoffs expanded to 10,000 from previously announced levels. The company simultaneously launched its first domestically produced recombinant PDRN product, reflecting a dual strategy of cost optimization and product innovation.

    Scoring Methodology

    Heat scores do not pursue absolute heat — they serve relative ranking among peer products. Core principle: only signals from the specific product SKU itself may be counted; brand heat must never substitute for product heat.

    Total Score Structure

    MVP stage uses 50/50.

    Sales Score Formula

    Sales Signal = Base × Platform Authority × Evidence Confidence × Time Decay
    Countable signals: Bestseller / ranking / monthly sales·GMV / Sold Out·Restock / Hot·Trending tags / multi-channel distribution / authoritative retail endorsement
    Evidence Confidence: explicit screenshot ×1.0 · platform official tag ×0.8 · third-party article mention ×0.6 · manual judgment ×0.4

    Buzz Score Formula

    Buzz Signal = Base × Platform Authority × Buzz Type Weight × Time Decay
    Buzz three-tier weight: Organic ×1.0 > Editorial ×0.75 > Paid/Seeded ×0.45
    Organic: user posts/comments/UGC/Reddit · Editorial: Allure/Vogue/NYMag/Cosmopolitan/Byrdie/WhoWhatWear · Paid: brand BGC/PR seeding/creator collab

    Time Decay

    0-30d ×1.0 · 31-90d ×0.8 · 91-180d ×0.6 · 181-365d ×0.4 · >1yr ×0.2
    Long-term bestsellers may retain higher weight with continued evidence.

    Display Score Tiers

    90-98 Viral · 85-89 Trending · 80-84 Strong Interest · 75-79 Rising · 70-74 Niche Signal · 65-69 Low Confidence
    Internal raw_score drives true ranking, never tampered; display_score capped at 98, floored at 65.

    Core rule: A product cannot score high simply because its brand is hot. Only SKU-level sales/ranking/editorial/user-discussion/social/restock evidence counts. Brand heat without product-level evidence → background info only, no heat added.

    Cross-panel consistency: Heat rankings and new-product radar use the same scoring formula and reporting-cycle data. The same product must have identical scores across both panels. The two panels differ only in product selection scope (Heat = market-wide TOP10 / Radar = last-4-week new launches); calculation is identical.
