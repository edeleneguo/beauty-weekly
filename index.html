CNProya Acquires Controlling Stake in Flower Knows for ~¥800M
Proya's subsidiary acquired an additional 12.55% stake in Flower Knows for ¥351M, raising total ownership to 51% for consolidation. Combined with the initial ¥428M investment, total commitment reaches ~¥800M. Flower Knows is known for its "girlhood aesthetic" and premium packaging.
GLOBALEstée Lauder × Puig Merger Talks: A Potential $40B Beauty Giant
Estée Lauder and Spain's Puig confirmed negotiations on a "potential business combination." If completed, the merged entity would have a ~$40B market cap and ~$20B annual revenue, becoming the world's second-largest beauty group after L'Oréal.

As China's 15th Five-Year Plan begins, 60+ new cosmetic regulations were issued in 2025, covering the full supply chain. 169 new cosmetic ingredient filings were approved, marking a new era of compliance-driven growth.
USUlta Q1 2026 Fragrance: DKNY Surges +58 Ranks, Kylie +24
Ulta Beauty Q1 2026 fragrance rankings: DKNY surged 58 positions to lead gains, followed by Kylie Cosmetics (+24) and touchland (+18). Consumer appetite for fragrance remains robust.
